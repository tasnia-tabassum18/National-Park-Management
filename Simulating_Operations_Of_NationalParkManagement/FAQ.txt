FAQ about National Park:
Q1:Is there any resthouse available in the park?
Ans:Unfortunately, resthouse is not available.However, there is waiting lounge available near the ticket counter.
Q2:Are pets allowed?
Ans:Due to safety reasons, pets are not allowed on the park.
Q3:Are you open on Fridays?
Ans:We are open everyday of the week from 9am to 6pm except Saturday.
